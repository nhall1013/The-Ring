The Alien

You reduced speed but you need to do some quick calculations to fix your trajectory!

Perform the following calculations:

  • 2 x 2
  • 4 / 2
  • 5 - 5
  • 6 + 2

You decide to keep these calculations close by. You have a feeling they could be useful later.

After figuring out your calculations you adjust your trajectory. Everything looks great. As you begin to approach the ring you get a strange feeling. It feels like someone is watching you. When you finally arrive at the ring a being emerges and approaches your ship! The being does not seem to be wearing any kind of protective space suit. Was this first contact with alien life? You're thrilled and terrified at the same time. At first glance it seems like the being is glowing. As it draws closer you hear a voice inside your head. The alien is speaking to you telepathically!

"You may not know my name but I know yours. We know why you've come here. If the human race interferes with our work again we will have no choice but to destroy Earth. We demand that you leave and never attempt to contact us again. But before we let you leave you must answer our questions!"

What do you do?


Fire up the ships engines and get out of there as fast as you can.
